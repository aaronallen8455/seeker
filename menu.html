Welcome


The goal of this game is to maneuver the cursor through the course without touching the walls, boundaries, or any other obstacles.

This alone can be challenging, but on top of that, each level also contains a malicious entity that will attempt to hunt you down.

To play, simply move the cursor over the 'Start' button then click and hold the mouse button down while you navigate towards the Finish. You can reset the level by pressing the Space Bar.
